Fun Facts

Impalas came in a full complement of body styles, and accounted for the lion's share of sales. Impala output, in fact, totaled 556,800 cars with V-8 power and 18,800 carrying six-cylinder engines

Top engine this season was a 385-horsepower 427-cubic-inch V-8. Of the 76,055 Impala SS models built, just 2,124 were SS 427 editions. Only about 400 Super Sports had a six-cylinder engine.

A black 1967 Sport Sedan 4-door hardtop was used in the television series Supernatural this model has seen a substantial increase in value to collectors.

The first year the Impala had a 327 V8 Turbo-Jet was in 1962. These engines also powered Corvettes, Novas, and Chevelles through the 1960s.
